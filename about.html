Solving digital problems with an outstanding creative flare

In 2016, I started working as Digital Designer and Front-End Developer for the ‘Virtus dizajn’ company where I was primarily responsible for Web Design and Applications.

Following this, I got a job at ‘Degordian’, one of the best digital agencies in Croatia. Working for Degordian was a great experience for me as the environment there was extremely professional, inspiring and creative. During that period I was working on Web Design, and Front-End development. One excellent experience I'm highly proud of was working as a Front-End Developer on applications for ‘Sheikh Abdullah Al Salem Cultural Center’, the largest museum in the world.

Our team designed and developed over 32 applications to help familiarize visitors of the museum with the history of the Earth. The applications involved a detailed research, UX/UI design and very interesting interactions and animations, providing a great opportunity to learn and progress.

For the last two years, I’ve been living in the UK, working with variety of clients, helping them get the best from the web. I was given the opportunity to work on some great project, by doing a research, user interviews, user testing, personas, storytelling, user flow, wireframes, before the final designs, applying the user-centred design methods.
